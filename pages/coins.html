About Asset

Bitcoin is the first successful internet money based on peer-to-peer technology; whereby no central bank or authority is involved in the transaction and production of the Bitcoin currency. It was created by an anonymous group under the name, Satoshi Nakamoto. The source code is available as an open source project for anybody to access. Bitcoin is changing the way we see money as you read this description.

The idea was to producr a means of exchange, independent of any central authority, that could be transferred electronically in a secure, verifiable and immutable way. It is a decentralised peer-to-peer internet currenct making mobule payment easy, very low transaction fees, protects your identity, and it works anywhere all the time with no central authority and banks.

Bitcoin is designed to have only 21 million BTC ever created, thus making it a deflationary currency.
